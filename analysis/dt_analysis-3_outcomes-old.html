Dog Trainer Questionnaire vs Outcome (GD, AD, W)

Marinara Marcato

Introduction

This document tests the hypothesis that there is a difference in behaviours reported in the standardised questionnaire Monash Canine Personality Questionnaire - Revised considering dog’s training outcome.

Data Analysis

Kruskal-Wallis one-way analysis of variance test was used to investigate whether there was a statistically significant difference between the behaviours reported by Trainers and the Training Outcome of the dogs. The variables that showed p_value < 0.05 are below:
